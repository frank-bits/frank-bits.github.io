Frequently asked questions

Can GardenPin map my garden?

Yes — GardenPin places each plant on an interactive GPS garden map, so you can see exactly where everything is planted across multiple gardens and beds.

Does GardenPin work offline?

Yes. Plant identification runs on-device with Core ML, so you can identify plants and manage your garden even without a signal.

Can I print plant labels?

Yes. GardenPin generates QR-coded plant labels you can AirPrint or send to a Brother label printer, linking each physical plant back to its care record.

Is GardenPin free?

GardenPin is free to download on the App Store. An optional Premium in-app purchase unlocks advanced features.
